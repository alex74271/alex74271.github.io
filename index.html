About Me.

I have experience doing science and communicating it. I'm interested in telling stories and making science more engaging.

The science community has its problems. It's afraid of narratives, scientists seem cold, and lay audiences have a hard time understanding them. I hope to fix that by telling the complete story of scientific findings (including all the failures), humanizing scientists, and conveying science in unique and comprehensible ways.
